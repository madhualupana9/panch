About Us Panchajanya Eco Villages
Panchajanya Ecovillages Pvt Ltd, founded by Mr.Rajender Reddy in 2009, has established itself as a leading provider of Villa Plots in Hyderabad, Shirdi, Bangalore, and Bangkok. Over the years, the company has sold thousands of plots, growing from its initial project of 2000 plots to delivering over 11 lakh square yards.

The company’s success is built upon its commitment to passing on the benefits of its cost-efficiency to its customers. This principle, along with the company’s constant efforts to improve the community and make a positive impact on people’s lives, has been a driving force behind its success over the last 25 years.

Our Story
Panchajanya Eco villages Pvt Ltd, founded by Mr.Rajender Reddy in 2009, has established itself as a leading provider of Villa Plots in Hyderabad, Shirdi, Bangalore, and Bangkok. Over the years, the company has sold thousands of plots, growing from its initial project of 2000 plots to delivering over 11 lakh square yards.

The company’s success is built upon its commitment to passing on the benefits of its cost-efficiency to its customers. This principle, along with the company’s constant efforts to improve the community and make a positive impact on people’s lives, has been a driving force behind its success over the last 25 years.

Our Vision
At Panchajanya Eco Villages, our vision is to create lasting value by empowering individuals and communities through the ownership of well-located, strategically developed open plots. We envision a future where everyone has the opportunity to own a piece of land that not only serves as a foundation for their dreams but also contributes to sustainable growth and development.

We are committed to transforming landscapes and elevating neighborhoods, offering transparent and reliable solutions that help you make informed decisions in the real estate market. Whether you're looking to build your dream home, invest in future growth, or create a legacy, we strive to make your journey as smooth and rewarding as possible.

Through innovation, integrity, and customer-centricity, we aim to shape a brighter, more prosperous future—one plot at a time.

In 2009, Mr. Dasarı founded Sahaja Properties, leveraging his expertise in the field and his entrepreneurial vision.
